Codebook
=========

These features are a transformation of the raw data. The Codebook for the original features is available here:
https://github.com/flysonic10/wearable-computing-data/blob/master/UCI%20HAR%20Dataset/features_info.txt

The transformations include:
  - The addition of Activity labels
  - The addition of Subject labels
  - Only mean and standard deviation variables were retained
  - The mean of all variables (mean and standard deviation variables) was taken across for each activity and each subject
  - Field names were kept similar to resemble the original data, however special characters were removed




##Fields  

1. Activity

  The name of 1 of the 6 activities for which the data was collected. These include:

        WALKING
        WALKING_UPSTAIRS
        WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
        SITTING
        STANDING
        LAYING

2. Subject
